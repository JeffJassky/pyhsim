04 / The standard

Built in the open. Every effect curve in the lab is traced to a published study. Every drug interaction is rule-coded by a pharmacist, not hallucinated by an LLM.

✓

Every effect has a citation.

Hover any biological effect in the model and you'll see the paper it came from. If we can't cite it, we don't model it.

✓

Confidence bands, not false certainty.

Every prediction is tagged with how strong the underlying evidence is. Rapamycin Q14d has weak human data; we say so.

✓

Drug interactions are rule-coded.

MAOIs + SSRIs. Methylene blue + serotonergics. Hard rules from pharmacist-reviewed sources, not statistical guesses.

✓

The model knows what it doesn't know.

Pregnant, breastfeeding, under 18, or stacking compounds with low literature — the lab flags it and refuses to overstate confidence.

✓

Not medical advice. By design.

Protokol Lab is an educational tool for self-experimentation. We're not a clinic. We don't want to be.
